About us

We're a family run business based at the South Yorkshire Fresh Produce & Flower Centre, just outside the centre of Sheffield.

We have been supplying the catering industry with quality packaging and catering supplies from some of the leading manuracturers for over 40 years.

You can view our full range of products below or use the navigation at the top of the page to find the products that you are looking for.

Eggs

Our eggs are sourced from local, reputable farms and are vacinated against salmonella and are all of Lion Quality standard.

We supply:

  • Free range eggs
  • Colony eggs
  • Goose eggs
  • Duck eggs
  • Quail eggs
  • Ostrich eggs
  • Egg boxes

All eggs can be packed on trays or pre-packed in 6's and 15's. We can supply single trays right through to full pallets and are all available at competitive wholesale prices.
